Features
Everything your society needs,
nothing it doesn't

Built specifically for savings societies and chit funds — not adapted from generic accounting software.

Multi-tenant by design
Each society gets their own isolated workspace. One subscription covers your entire org — all members, all branches.
Approval workflows
Large disbursements require 2-of-3 signatory sign-off. Treasurer, Secretary and President sign digitally — notified via WhatsApp.
Instant financial reports
Receipts & Payments, P&L, and Balance Sheet auto-generated in real time. Print-ready for AGMs and auditors.
Member self-service portal
Members log in to view their own contribution history, share count, loan status, and download receipts — without bothering the treasurer.
Works on any device
Fully responsive — treasurers record entries on desktop, members check balances on mobile. No app to install.
WhatsApp notifications
Payment confirmations, approval requests, and monthly summaries sent directly to members' WhatsApp — no email required.
How it works
Up and running in minutes
01
Create your society
Sign up, enter your society name, FY start date, and contribution amount. Invite your committee members by phone number.
02
Record every transaction
Log entrance fees, monthly contributions, loan disbursements, and repayments. The daybook tracks running balance automatically.
03
Share reports instantly
One click generates a print-ready R&P, P&L, or Balance Sheet. Share the link with your auditor or table it at the AGM.
